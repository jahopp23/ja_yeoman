About

I have always been interested in computers, media, visual arts, and culture. During University, I studied the paradigm of technology and human society while getting a degree in Anthropology. My college education proved to me that programming, computer software, and web development are the modern day pinnacle activities. With coding, the world is your oyster, and the developer is likened to a painter with a fresh canvas.

I wanted to pursue web development full-time, so I decided to receive professional training from Coding Dojo (www.codingdojo.com) in Dallas, Texas. During this time at Coding Dojo, I gained the modern coding tools necessary for the creation of full-stack web applications, mobile-responsive sites, UI/UX design, and how to transform brilliant ideas into code.

Afterwards, I went on to work as a Front-End Web Developer for an award-winning advertising agency in Dallas - Johnson & Sekin, where I tandemly developed two premiere client websites, mexene.com and teasdalelatinfoods.com. Furthermore, I have created numerous projects with a wide array of code and frameworks, as well as developing projects daily. Currently, I seek a position as Front-End Web Developer, UI Developer, or UI/UX role. My preferences for visual aesthetics, design, engineerning, and the web, render me a passionate and versatile developer.

Received the highest certification from Coding Dojo in Ruby On Rails - Black Belt
